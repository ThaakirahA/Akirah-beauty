Application Guide

How to Apply

01
Measure & Trim

Hold the lash against your eye and trim any excess from the outer edge to fit your lash line perfectly.

02
Apply Glue

Apply a thin layer of lash glue along the band. Wait 30 seconds until the glue becomes tacky before applying.

03
Press & Secure

Place the lash as close to your natural lash line as possible. Press the inner and outer corners first, then the middle.

04
Blend & Finish

Gently press your natural and false lashes together. Apply a thin line of liner to conceal the band for a seamless look.
