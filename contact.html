Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I place an order?

The easiest way is via WhatsApp — just message us with the product names and your delivery address. We'll confirm availability and payment details right away.

Do you deliver across South Africa?

Yes! We deliver nationwide. Delivery typically takes 2–4 business days. Free delivery applies on all orders over R300.

How do I build a custom box?

Visit our Custom Boxes page to browse available items, then send us your selections via WhatsApp. We'll price it up and pack it for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT (bank transfer) and cash on delivery in select areas. Payment details are confirmed via WhatsApp when you place your order.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Due to the nature of beauty products, we don't accept returns on opened items. If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 24 hours with a photo.

Are your lashes reusable?

Yes — our lashes are reusable up to 20–25 times with proper care. Remove gently, clean the band after each use, and store in the original packaging.
